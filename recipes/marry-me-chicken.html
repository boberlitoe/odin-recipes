Marry Me Chicken Recipe

Description

Marry me chicken is a creamy chicken dish made with seared chicken, garlic, parmesan, heavy cream, sun-dried tomatoes, and herbs. It’s rich, savory, slightly tangy, and usually served with pasta, rice, mashed potatoes, or bread. The name comes from the idea that it tastes so good someone would “marry you” after eating it.

The sauce is the star of the dish, coating the chicken in a smooth, flavorful blend that feels comforting but still a little fancy. The sun-dried tomatoes add a sweet and tangy bite, while the parmesan and cream make it rich and satisfying. It’s the kind of meal that works well for a cozy dinner, date night, or whenever you want something simple that feels special.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Flour, salt, and pepper
  • Olive oil and butter
  • Garlic
  • Chicken or vegetable stock
  • Heavy Cream and parmesan cheese
  • Chili flakes, dried oregano, and thyme
  • Sundried tomatoes
  • Fresh basil leaves

How to make it

  1. Dredge the chicken in flour.
  2. Heat oil in heavy bottom pan and melt butter in.
  3. Add in and brown the chicken, once its brown, remove and place onto a warm plate and cove it.
  4. Into the now empty skillet, add and saute the garlic until its fragrant.
  5. Add your stock and deglaze the pan with a wooden utensil.
  6. Add the heavy cream and parmesan cheese.
  7. This will create a sauce which you will now allow to simmer for a couple of minutes.
  8. Add chili flakes and oregano.
  9. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to your liking and add the sundried tomatoes.
  10. Put browned chicken back in and let the sauce simmer and thicken for a few more minutes

Congratulations! You now have Marry Me Chicken!
